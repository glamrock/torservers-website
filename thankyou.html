All donations will be used in full to cover operational costs of Tor exit nodes. If you donate more than $20 monthly, you are entitled to get your very own Tor exit node: Contact us and we'll set up a node carrying a name of your own choice.

Bandwidth stats are available (independently controlled by TorStatus), so you will be able to see what's happening with your money!
