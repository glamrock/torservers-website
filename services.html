Tor Exit Node Hosting

Without our servers, roughly 25% of all exit traffic in the Tor network passes through one node, which is far from ideal. The currently fastest node, Blutmagie, will be shut down within the next months, and the whole network and its users will suffer. We run high bandwidth Tor exit nodes - some of the fastest nodes in existence - and several small nodes. This helps to make the Tor network more stable, faster and more anonymous.

Private Bridge IP addresses

Bridges can be used to access the Tor network if public entry nodes are blocked. The Tor website offers a limited number of bridge addresses, but they might get blocked fast (which is happening in China, for example), or monitored to track down its users.

Thanks to funding by Access Now and others, we can provide exclusive free private bridge IP addresses to individuals and organizations.
To apply, drop us an anonymous mail. Tell us as many details as possible (no names required), and don't forget to include how we can reach you.

Hidden Services & Eepsite Hosting

In the future, we want to provide preconfigured, disk encrypted space for Tor hidden services and I2P eepsite hosting.
