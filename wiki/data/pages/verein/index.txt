====== Zwiebelfreunde ("Friends of the Onion") ======
  * [[verein:satzung|Satzung]]

===== Mission Statement =====

Cryptography, security, privacy and anonymity are public goods. They must not be a secret limited to experts and criminals. Members of Zwiebelfreunde ("Friends of the Onion") educate the public on how to professionally use and offer privacy related services.

Torservers.net is the first instance of our vision of a large network of separate, independent entities running Tor exits. Our goal is to "franchise" the Torservers concept by providing a complete set of guides ranging from legal advice to technical details. We especially want to foster [[:usergroups|local user groups]] to discuss decentralized structures and privacy.

If you want to help, we have a lot of open positions and [[:todo|todos]]. Don't be shy, find us on IRC.

This is just the beginning.

===== Ziele des Vereins =====

  * verlässliche Partner für Tor Exits ausfindig machen; diese Informationen allen Interessenten zugänglich machen
  * eigene Server betreiben
  * Ansprechpartner für Medien und sonstige Interessenten bieten
  * Tor Nutzergruppen/"Freunde" zusammenbringen (lokale Gruppen, globale Vernetzung)
  * Torservers replizierbar machen -> mehr Dezentralität
